Faux Wood Blinds
For a more budget-friendly option, faux wood blinds provide the same stylish appearance as wood but are more resistant to moisture. These blinds are an excellent choice for Clermont Florida homes near coastal areas, as they are not prone to warping or cracking due to humidity.

Clermont, FL Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Clermont is a city in Lake County in central Florida, United States, about 22 miles (35 km) west of Orlando and 22 miles (35 km) southeast of Leesburg. The population was 43,021 in 2020.
